About Our Network

East Coast Trade Network (ECTN) is a South Africa–based export company specializing in the global trade of high-quality commodities across multiple sectors. We connect local producers with verified international buyers, facilitating efficient, transparent, and compliant trade operations. Our mission is to position African commodities on the world stage by ensuring quality, reliability, and long-term partnerships.

Our Commodities

  • Mineral Exports: Chrome ore, manganese, iron ore, and other mineral sands.
  • Agricultural Exports: Fruits, vegetables, nuts, and sugar.
  • Bulk Commodities: Grains, pulses, and other essential raw materials.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be Africa’s most trusted bridge between local producers and global markets.

Mission: To empower trade growth through verified sourcing, world-class logistics, and unwavering integrity.
